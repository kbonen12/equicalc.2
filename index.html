Ketamine (100 mg/mL)–

Infusion Rate–
Standard GKX: 1 L guaifenesin + 2 g ketamine + 500 mg xylazine. Rate: 1–2 mL/kg/hr (55–110 mL/hr for 500 kg). Supplement with inhalant if prolonged.
